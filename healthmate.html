Stay Hydrated
Drink at least 8 glasses of water throughout the day. Proper hydration boosts focus, energy, and mood during long study sessions.
Physical Health

Eat Your Greens
Add at least one serving of vegetables to every meal. Nutrients from greens support brain function and keep your immune system strong.
Nutrition

10-Minute Walk
A short walk between classes reduces anxiety and refreshes your mind. Even campus hallways count — just move your body!

Sleep 7–9 Hours
Quality sleep consolidates memory, balances stress hormones, and is essential for academic performance especially during exam season.
Sleep
🤝
Connect Socially
Spend 15 minutes with a friend or classmate. Social connection is one of the strongest predictors of mental wellness for college students.
Mental Health

Take a 30-minute break from all screens. Reduces eye strain and mental fatigue, giving your brain time to rest and reset between tasks.
